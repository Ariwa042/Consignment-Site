Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that you may have questions about our services. Below, you’ll find answers to some of the most common inquiries from our customers. If you need further assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team!

What shipping options do you offer?

We offer a variety of shipping options, including standard shipping for cost-effective deliveries, express shipping for urgent packages, and international shipping for global reach. Each option is designed to meet your specific needs.

How can I track my shipment?

You can easily track your shipment using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Simply visit our tracking page, enter your tracking number, and you’ll see real-time updates on your package's status and location.

What should I do if my package is delayed?

If your package is delayed, please check the tracking information for updates. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our customer support team. We are here to help you resolve any issues as quickly as possible.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Yes, you can change your shipping address as long as your order has not yet been shipped. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible to update your shipping information.

What if my package is lost or damaged?

If your package is lost or damaged, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will investigate the issue and work with you to find a resolution, which may include a replacement or refund, depending on the circumstances.

Can I schedule a pick-up for my shipment?

Yes, you can schedule a pick-up for your shipment through our online portal or by contacting our customer service. We offer flexible pick-up options to accommodate your needs.

How do I prepare my items for shipping?

Ensure your items are securely packaged to prevent damage during transit. Use appropriate packing materials, such as bubble wrap and sturdy boxes. Label your package clearly with the recipient's address and include any necessary documentation.
